Factors that Influence Bike Share Trip Duration in the San Francisco Bay Area¶

by BeRT2me¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the variables that influence drip duration. The main factors found to have an influence were weather, age, and gender proportion.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted of daily summaries of rideshare and weather data from June 28th 2017 to April 30th 2019.

Temperature and Trip Duration on Weekends¶

This graph scatter plots the average temperatures and average trip duration in minutes on weekends.
There is also a line of best fit that highlights the general correlation of the data.

This highlights how as Temperature increases, so does the Trip Duration.
